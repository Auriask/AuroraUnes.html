About the university

Aurora UNES University is a renowned institution of higher learning committed to fostering academic excellence, innovation, and personal growth. Founded in 2025, our university has a rich history of providing top-quality education and a vibrant campus life. Situated on a picturesque campus spanning 5 acres in the heart of Moncton, Aurora UNES University offers students a serene and conducive environment for learning.

Our commitment to academic excellence is unwavering. Aurora UNES University boasts a distinguished faculty of experts who are leaders in their respective fields. Our diverse range of undergraduate and graduate programs ensures that students can pursue their passions and gain the knowledge and skills needed for success in their chosen careers.
